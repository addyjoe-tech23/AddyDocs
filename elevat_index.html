Meet APOSTLE PERNELL ROBERT OSEI-BOAKYE, ESQ.
Board Secretary | Legal Practitioner | Chartered Banker | Lecturer | Pastor | Researcher
Apostle Pernell Robert Osei-Boakye, Esq. is a seasoned Legal Practitioner, Chartered Banker, and Theologian
with over three decades of leadership experience spanning banking, law, academia, and ministry.
He currently serves as the Board Secretary of Ada Rural Bank PLC, Ghana,
and practices law as a Senior Associate at The Law Office of Kwainoe,Accra.
In addition, he lectures Commercial Law at MountCrest University College, Accra,
where he trains future legal professionals in business and corporate law.
